Analytics

Most of my skills with data science come from being a broke college student. Data on financial instruments can sell for hundreds of dollars online. As an undergraduate researcher, my school gave me a budget of ~$600. which was spent almost entirely on books and server time. I needed a way to get the data myself.

At the time, IEX ofference a free API which would give you a days worth of minute-interval for any ticker you asked < for. The major downside of this was that you could only pull data on one company, from one day, with each request Fortunately they did not have an rate limits. I wrote a program which read a list of N ticker symbols and pulled data for each day available. Once I got this data into csv form I then had to clean it.

Companies who have provided free data APIs in the past (Morninstar, IEX, Google, Yahoo), have all transitioned from to a paid service (IEX and Morningstar,), or no longer offering the service in any useful way (Yahoo and Google).
